How to play

Welcome to Time Score, a game that tests both your typing speed and your knowledge of different categories.

Select a game to play from the game list page. Make sure you read the game specific instructions on the game information page, they will help you. Press start when you are ready and start typing! Any correct answers will automatically be accepted!

If you are logged in, you will be able to create games of your own and record your high scores

Good Luck!
